Emergency Go Bag

What to pack


.

FOOD AND WATER

Bring clean drinking water and long-lasting food like canned goods or energy bars for survival during emergencies.

.

FIRST AID KIT

Include basic medical items like bandages, antiseptic, and medicine for small injuries.

.

FLASHLIGHT & WHISTLE

A flashlight helps you see in the dark. A whistle can help you call for help if needed.

.

EXTRA CLOTHES

Pack spare clothes to keep yourself warm, dry, and comfortable if you have to leave your home.
